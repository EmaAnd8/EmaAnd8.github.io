I’m Emanuele Andaloro, a Master’s candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at Politecnico di Milano, where I’m also vying to become a PoliMi Ambassador for Smart Infrastructures. With a first-class Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Università di Catania, I thrive at the intersection of blockchain, cybersecurity, and hardware acceleration. Curious and adaptable, I’ve led and contributed to projects ranging from AI-driven well-being apps to deploying CNNs and custom ViTs on embedded NPUs. Outside of academics, I mentor middle-school students through Fondazione Cariplo’s TOP program and volunteer as a blood donor with AVIS. Feel free to connect on LinkedIn or explore my work on GitHub.

Embedding Deep Learning on STM32N6 Edge Devices

Deployed and benchmarked quantized CNNs (YOLO v1, custom YOLO v5n) and a compact Vision Transformer on an STM32N6570-DK using ST’s Cube.AI toolchain. Achieved up to 510 FPS object detection with sub-2 ms latency on the NPU and analyzed trade-offs between accuracy, throughput, and model size.
